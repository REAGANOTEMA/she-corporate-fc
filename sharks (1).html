About the Club

She Corporate FC is a leading force in Ugandan women's football, known as 'The Sharks' and 'The Inspirers'. Competing in the FUFA Women Super League, we have a rich history of success, including the 2021/22 FUFA Women Super League title, 2023/24 FUFA Women Elite League championship, and 2023/24 FUFA Women's Cup victory. Our club is committed to empowering women through sport, fostering talent, and building a strong community. With professional standards, tactical organization, and a focus on defensive discipline, we continue to produce national team players and inspire the next generation.

Club History

She Corporate FC competes in the FUFA Women Super League and is nicknamed “The Sharks.” The club featured in the inaugural women’s league in 2015 and has remained a pillar of Ugandan women’s football.

After a strong resurgence, She Corporate won the FUFA Women Elite League—earning promotion back to the Super League—and later secured the 2024 FUFA Women’s Cup in a penalty shootout against Rines SS WFC, underscoring their competitive edge and resilience.

The team is widely recognized for defensive discipline, tactical organization, and professional standards, consistently producing national‑team players and maintaining a strong presence in the league’s top tier.
